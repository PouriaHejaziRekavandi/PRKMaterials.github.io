Welcome to RKV Materials

Welcome to the official page of Pouria Hejazi Rekavandi. Our research group focuses on the development, numerical analysis, and optimization of novel engineering structures.

Our main objective is to bridge theoretical concepts, computational codes, and advanced modeling to address contemporary challenges in materials and mechanical engineering, including the study of composite sandwich panels and bio-inspired helicoidal structures.
