Primary Research Areas

Composite Sandwich Panels

Study and optimization of composite sandwich panels featuring various cores, specifically focusing on honeycomb configurations under uniform external compressive load.

Bio-inspired Composites

Design, data extraction, and structural analysis of bio-inspired helicoidal composite structures to significantly enhance impact resistance and load-bearing capacity.

Computational Modeling

Development and debugging of computational codes in MATLAB, alongside utilizing finite element methods for precise simulation of complex mechanical behaviors.
